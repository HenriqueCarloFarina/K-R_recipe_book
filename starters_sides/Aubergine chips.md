
Ingredients
Aubergine
Smoked paprika
Cumin
Garlic granules
Salt and pepper
Parsley 
Parmesan
Olive oil

Garlicky sauce
Yogurt 
Lemon juice
Fresh garlic (little )
Salt and peper

Method
Chop Aubergine into thick chips
Coat Aubergine in spices and oil
Airfry for 15 mins, max crisp for 2 minutes
Coat in chopped parsley and grated parmesan
