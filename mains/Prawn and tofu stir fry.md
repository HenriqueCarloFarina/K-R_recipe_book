### Asian prawn and tofu stir fry

![[prawn_tofu_stir_fry.jpg]]

#main #asian

**Ingredients:**
- Prawns  
- Tofu  
- Onion  
- Clove garlic  
- Coriander chopped  
- Crushed peanuts  

**Sauce:**  
- Half a lime juice  
- Teaspoon miso paste  
- Half teaspoon tamarind paste  
- Pinch chilli flakes  
- Some sesame oil  
  
**Method:**
- Fry tofu and onion  
- Add garlic and prawns  
- Add sauce
