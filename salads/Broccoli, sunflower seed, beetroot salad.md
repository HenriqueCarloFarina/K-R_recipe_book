### Broccoli, sunflower seed, beetroot salad

#salad 

**Ingredients:**
- Broccoli  
- Cooked beetroot  
- Salad leaves  
- Sunflower seeds  

**Dressing: **
- Dijon mustard
- mayonnaise
- cider vinegar 
- olive oil
- salt and peper

**Method:**
- Chop broccoli into small florets  
- Steam broccoli  
- Chop beetroot and add to salad leaves  
- Toast sunflower seeds in dry pan  
- Make dressing
