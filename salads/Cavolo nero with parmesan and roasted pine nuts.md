### Cavolo nero with parmesan and roasted pine nuts

#Salad

![[cavolo_nero_salad1.png|300]]  

**Ingredients**:
- Cavolo Nero  
- Parmesan  
- Pine nuts  
- Olive oil  
- Pepper

**Methods**
1) Steam cavolo nero  
2) Toast pine nuts in dry pan  
3) Add grated parmesan, olive oil and pepper to cavolo nero
