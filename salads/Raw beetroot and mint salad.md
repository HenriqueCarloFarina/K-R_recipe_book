### Raw beetroot and mint salad

![alt](/z_imgs/raw_beetroot.jpg)

#salad 

**Ingredients:**
- Raw beetroot  
- Mint  
- Olive oil  
- Lemon  
- Salt and pepper

**Methods:**
- Wash beetroot  
- Shave slices  
- Arrange on plate with chopped mint  
- Squeeze lemon and drizzle olive oil over  
- Add salt and pepper
